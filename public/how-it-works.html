How PetSwap Works

Four simple steps to cheaper holidays and happier pets

1

Create Your Profile

Tell us about your home, your pets, and when you like to travel. Add photos to showcase your place.

2

Find Your Match

Browse other pet-loving families. Filter by location, pet type, and dates. Send requests to those you like.

3

Connect & Agree

Message through our secure system. Ask questions. Agree on dates and house rules.

4

Swap & Enjoy

Exchange keys (or codes). Enjoy your holiday knowing your pets are happy at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! The first 100 members get free lifetime membership. After that, it's just $9.99/month.

Do I have to swap at the same time?

Not at all. Use our GuestPoints system to host without staying, then redeem points later for your own trip.

What about insurance?

We recommend our partner insurers for peace of mind. Exchange details are shared only after both parties agree.

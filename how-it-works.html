‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! The first 100 members get free lifetime membership. After that, it's just $9.99/month or $99/year for premium features.

Do I have to swap at the same time?

Not necessarily! We offer "GuestPoints" - you can host other members without staying at their place, and use those points later for your own trip.

What if something goes wrong?

We have 24/7 support and a dispute resolution process. For added peace of mind, we recommend our partner insurance for exchanges.

What about my pets?

That's the best part! Your pets stay in their familiar home, with another loving family to care for them. No stress for your furry friends.

Can I exchange with multiple families?

Absolutely! You can have ongoing exchanges with several families, giving you flexibility for different trips.

What if I have exotic pets?

We welcome all pets! Just be detailed in your pet profile about care requirements, and find exchange partners who are comfortable with your type of pet.
